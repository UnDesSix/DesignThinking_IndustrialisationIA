# Gavin Johnson
After the success of his exhibition at COYA City, Gavin Johnson has unveiled a new, exclusive collection now showcased at COYA Mayfair.
From now until the end of December, immerse yourself in Gavin's innovative artwork, which draws on psychological insights and the dynamics of human experience. His work seamlessly blends organic and geometric elements, offering a vibrant and thought-provoking visual experience.
Curated and exhibited by Alinda Kring Art Advisory.
