## Israeli Specialities
Israeli cuisine is unique and combines influences from many places. It is a spiritual and culinary fusion; a combination of flavours that awaken the palate. This cuisine is characterised by exotic herbs and spices, meat, fish, cheese and many nutritious ingredients. Typical dishes include hummus, fattoush salad, shawarmas, bourekas, falafel and mezze. Israeli cuisine's hallmarks are a variety of flavours and textures. The dishes often feature olive oil, herbs and spices, and many are also vegan or vegetarian.
